---

## FORMAT RULES

- Size: `1080 x 1350`
- Safe text width: `820 px`
- Safe top/bottom margin: `120 px`
- Headline max: `3 lines`
- Body max: `5 short lines`
- Use screenshots only when they add status or credibility
- Every slide must read instantly on a phone

---

## TYPE SYSTEM

- Eyebrow / labels: mono, uppercase, tracked
- Headline: oversized display, hard stop, short lines
- Body: direct, compact, high contrast
- CTA / footer: restrained mono

Recommended proportions:
- Eyebrow: `26–32 px`
- Headline: `110–150 px`
- Body: `38–46 px`
- Fine note: `24–28 px`

The energy should come from rhythm and scale, not clutter.

## DESIGN NOTES FOR THE CREATIVE

### 1. Premium first
Do not let hype turn into noise.
The confidence should feel expensive.

### 2. Hype through scale
Use stronger headlines, not more decoration.

### 3. Screenshots should prove, not clutter
Only use UI where it clearly strengthens the story.

### 4. Keep the palette disciplined
- background: near-black / deep navy
- text: warm white
- accent: muted gold only

### 5. Best slide treatment
- slide 1, 2, 5, and 8 should be mostly typography
- slide 3 and 6 can carry the best UI crops
- slide 7 should feel like the product just crossed a threshold
