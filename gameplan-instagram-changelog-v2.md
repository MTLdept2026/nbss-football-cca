## POSTING NOTES
- **Best time**: Tuesday or Thursday, 7–9pm SGT (after training / dinner scroll)
- **Carousel tip**: Feature 01 (Share) and Feature 03 (Lineup + Story Export) are the two that
  will drive the most curiosity — consider running those as separate Reels/Stories first to prime
  the audience before the carousel drops.
- **Reel option**: Screen-record the Share button flow (tap panel → native share sheet → sent to
  WhatsApp). Under 15s. Add the cluster attendance table as a second cut if you want coaching
  staff to share it upward to the TIC.
- **Story companion**: Poll sticker — *"Which upgrade do you actually want to use?"*
  Options: Share Button vs Lineup Builder. Drives opens.
- **Pinned comment**: "Tag a player you want to see their ACWR 👀" — low effort, high engagement.

## WHAT'S NOT IN THIS POST (HONEST NOTES)
These features shipped but are too granular for Instagram:
- Draft auto-save on all forms (session, wellness, match, lineup — data survives a page refresh)
- Cookie-based access gate replacing localStorage (makes it work properly when added to home
  screen as a PWA on iOS/Android — worth a Story for your tech-savvy players)
- Playfair Display serif font for coaching directives (editorial detail — visible in the
  coaching insight panels)
- WCAG AA contrast compliance on light mode gold (accessibility — not a visual hook but the
  right call)
- Image compression on profile photos (UX polish)
- ReferenceArea on load charts (for coaches doing ACWR zone visualisation — niche but useful)
