## POSTING NOTES
- **Best time to post**: Tuesday or Thursday, 7–9pm SGT (after training / dinner scroll)
- **Story companion**: Screenshot each slide and add a poll sticker — *"Which upgrade do you care about most?"* with Alerts vs Light Mode. Gets engagement + tells you what to double down on next.
- **Pinned comment idea**: "Drop your ACWR below 👇 let's see who's in the optimal zone" — drives opens and data logging.
- **Reel option**: Screen-record flipping through the updated app with a trending sound. 15s is enough. Light mode toggle reveal is the money shot.
